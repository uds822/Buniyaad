Procedure

Students seeking admission for any of our courses (from Class 6th to 12th) must take an admission test, which is based on the NCERT syllabus for their respective class (State Board and CBSE).

Registration

Students can register for admission online or offline. The admission test can be taken online, and for the offline test, please contact our office.

Admission Test

Students who qualify with a minimum of 70% on the admission test can enroll in any of our courses. This is a mandatory requirement for all applicants.

Any Enquiry

For any admission-related queries, feel free to contact us at +91-7808454171 or email us at buniyaadthefoundation@gmail.com. We're always here to assist you!
